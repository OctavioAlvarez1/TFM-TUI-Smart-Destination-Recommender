# Business Problem and Vision

## Horizon — Intelligent Platform for Sustainable Tourist Demand Redistribution in Spain

---

# 1. Business Problem Definition

Spain is one of the most visited tourist destinations in the world. However, tourist demand is concentrated in a small number of destinations such as Barcelona, Mallorca, Ibiza, Tenerife, and the Costa del Sol. Approximately 85% of visitors go to just 10% of destinations.

This concentration creates a series of challenges affecting both local communities and the long-term sustainability of the tourism sector.

## Environmental Challenges

- Saturation of beaches and natural spaces.
- Increased pressure on water and energy resources.
- Higher waste generation.
- Deterioration of local ecosystems.

## Social Challenges

- Congestion of urban infrastructure.
- Rising cost of living for residents.
- Gentrification of tourist areas.
- Growing social rejection of mass tourism.

## Economic Challenges

- Excessive dependence on a small group of destinations.
- Underutilization of tourism infrastructure in less-visited regions.
- Unequal distribution of the economic benefits of tourism.

## Opportunity for TUI

TUI holds large volumes of data related to bookings, accommodations, destinations, activities, and traveler preferences.

By applying Artificial Intelligence, TUI can positively influence a traveler's decision before a booking is made, promoting alternative destinations that deliver a comparable experience while reducing pressure on the most congested destinations.

# 2. Solution Objectives

## Primary Objective

Redistribute a portion of tourist demand toward less congested destinations without negatively affecting customer experience.

## Secondary Objectives

### Sustainability

# 7. MVP Functional Scope

The MVP is fully built and operational as of June 2026. The following capabilities have been implemented using Python, FastAPI, Pandas, React 19, TypeScript, Vite, and Material UI v9.

## Implemented Features

### Recommendation Engine

Generates personalized destinations for each user using a weighted scoring formula:

```
Final Score = 0.45 × Preference + 0.25 × Sustainability + 0.15 × Popularity + 0.15 × Congestion
```

Business rules (multiplicative):
- Sustainability > 85 → ×1.05 (green boost)
- Sustainability < 50 → ×0.90 (penalty)
- Congestion < 40 → ×1.05 (under-visited bonus)
- Congestion > 80 → ×0.90 (redistribution penalty)

### User Profiling

Profiles built from declared preferences and historical booking behavior across 100 synthetic GDPR-compliant traveler profiles.

### Tourist Congestion Index

Monthly congestion pressure calculated per destination using INE EOH (Table 49371) and FRONTUR (Table 23988) open data. Covers 20 destinations with 12 monthly congestion values each (240 records total).

### Hybrid Ranking Engine

Combines preference affinity, sustainability scoring, popularity, and congestion data into a single ranked list of recommendations.

### Frontend — Four Fully Built Pages

1. **Destinations (Home)** — search form, recommendation cards with score breakdown, KPI dashboard.
2. **Insights** — Leaflet interactive map, Low Season Optimizer, monthly congestion heatmap, redistribution scenarios.
3. **Analytics** — governance dashboard, penalized destinations chart, status breakdown, filterable table.
4. **About** — project context, animated scoring formula, 5-layer architecture, project scope.

### Impact Dashboard

Streamlit-based dashboard (separate optional process) for visualizing redistribution and sustainability metrics.

# 8. Executive Summary

**Horizon** (TUI Smart Destination Recommender) is a fully operational full-stack AI system designed to promote more sustainable tourism in Spain by intelligently redistributing tourist demand.

The solution combines traveler preferences, sustainability indicators, popularity data, and congestion measurements to recommend personalized alternatives that maintain a satisfying customer experience, reduce pressure on saturated destinations, and promote a more balanced distribution of the economic benefits of tourism.

The MVP covers 20 Spanish destinations, 100 synthetic user profiles, approximately 1,000 booking records, and integrates open data from INE and FRONTUR. It is accessible through a React 19 single-page application backed by a FastAPI REST API.
